ONLINE APPOINTMENT SYSTEM FOR CLINICS AND DOCTORS

This system facilitates the appointment process for health professionals. Offer your patients the ability to make online appointments with you from every computer and mobile device connected to the internet 24/7. Managing your appointments and scheduless makes your health center a streamlined organization which works on an efficient schedule that makes full use of your resources and improves the well being of your patients.  Online scheduling simplifies communication between doctors and patients through simplified online scheduling.

SERVICES AND FEATURES

Clinic Management

Every clinic can sign up to this system which will enable them to add their doctors to their clinics, manage patients, history, booking and offer prescriptions

Appointments

Create, set and schedule automated medical appointment reminders via sms or email. Inform your doctors, assistants or other professionals in your organization about new appointments, changes in appointment details or cancellation of appointments. Set appointment reminders for patient to avoid the waste of valuable time of doctors by no-shows. 

History

By using bookitit as your appointment manager you automatically keep track of your patients in your database. You can add their medical record, personal details, contact information and send notifications. When patients make their first appointment with you, online or manually, their data is automatically saved into your database.
